119.5 SECONDS & 232 DEGREES

Joost went to Dublin with a friend in the summer of 2017, where he went to the Guinness Storehouse. There he encountered several ‘Fun Facts™’ that Joost all told at people present at Sam’s last day at Café Hoegaarden.
The 119.5 seconds was one of Joost’s many fun facts. It takes exactly this much time to pour a perfect pint of Guinness.
The 232 degrees was yet another of Joost’s many fun facts. It is the exact temperature (C) that the Barley, one of the ingredients in Guinness, is roasted to get it’s perfect color and texture.
